How We Work

Simple, transparent 4-step process. No surprises, no delays — just clear progress from first message to live website.

4-Step Process

From First Message to Live Website

Basic plan: 7 working days. Standard plan: 10–14 working days. Timeline starts once content and advance payment are received.

Day 1 — Free

01 / Consultation

We start with a simple WhatsApp or phone conversation to understand your business, goals, and budget. We'll recommend the right plan and give you a clear written quote with no hidden charges.

Days 2–7 (Basic) / 2–14 (Standard)

02 / Development

After 50% advance, we begin building. We request your content via WhatsApp — logo, photos, text. A design preview link is shared within 4–5 days so you can give early feedback.

Runs alongside development

03 / Optimization

Before launch we check page speed, mobile display, SEO tags, forms, and cross-device testing. Every site goes through this checklist — no exceptions.

Day 7–14 onwards

04 / Launch & Support

Final approval, remaining payment, then we go live. Domain + hosting setup assisted. Full credentials handed to you. Post-launch support: 7 days (Basic) or 30 days (Standard).

Typical Timeline at a Glance

Day 1
Consultation & quote. 50% advance payment to confirm project.
Day 4–5
Design preview shared via link for your feedback.
Day 5–12
Revisions done. Full website ready for your final review.
Day 7–14
Final approval, payment, domain setup — website goes live.

Timelines may vary if content (text, photos, logo) is delayed on the client's end. We always communicate openly about any changes.
