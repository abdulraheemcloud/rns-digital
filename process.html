How We Work

A simple, transparent 4-step process from your first message to a live website. No surprises, no delays — just clear progress.

The Process

From Conversation to Live Website

Most projects are completed within 10–18 working days depending on the plan and how quickly you share your content and feedback.

01

Consultation

Day 1 – Free, No Commitment

We start with a simple conversation — over WhatsApp, phone, or a brief video call. We want to understand your business, your customers, and what you need your website to do.

We'll ask about your business type, your competitors, your goals, and your budget. Based on this, we'll recommend the right plan and discuss realistic expectations.

Plan recommendation (Basic or Growth)
Clear project scope & timeline
Written quote with no hidden charges
02

Development

Days 2–10 (Basic) / Days 2–15 (Growth)

Once you confirm and pay the 50% advance, we begin building. We'll request your content (logo, photos, text, services list) via WhatsApp. If you don't have content ready, we'll guide you on what's needed.

We send you a design preview link before completing the full build, so you can provide feedback early. This ensures there are no major surprises at the end.

Design preview within 4–5 working days
Full website development with revisions
WhatsApp updates at every milestone
03

Optimization

Days 10–15 (runs alongside development)

Before launch, we run through a quality checklist — page speed, mobile responsiveness, SEO meta tags, broken links, form testing, and cross-browser checks. This is not optional; it's part of every project.

Page speed optimization (image compression, clean code)
SEO meta tags, sitemap, robots.txt
Mobile & desktop testing on real devices
Contact form & WhatsApp link testing
04

Launch & Support

Day 15–18 onwards

Once you approve the final website and complete the remaining 50% payment, we go live. We'll help you connect your domain, set up your hosting, and point everything correctly so the launch is smooth.

After launch, you get 14 days (Basic) or 30 days (Growth) of post-launch support for any issues or minor tweaks. We'll also hand over all credentials and documentation so you're fully independent.

Domain + hosting setup assistance
Full credential handover (you own everything)
Post-launch support window
Optional monthly support plans available

Typical Project Timeline

Day 1
Consultation & project confirmation. 50% advance payment.
Day 4–5
Design preview shared via link for your feedback.
Day 8–12
Revisions completed. Full website ready for review.
Day 14–18
Final approval, remaining payment, and website goes live.

Note: Timelines may vary if content (text, photos, logo) is delayed on the client's end. We'll always communicate openly if there are any delays.
