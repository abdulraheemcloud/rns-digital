Ideal For

Who Should Choose the Basic Plan?

The Basic Plan is the right fit if any of these describe your situation:

You're a small local business (restaurant, clinic, salon, shop, coaching class) that currently has no website or a very outdated one.
Your main goal is establishing an online presence so customers can find you on Google and verify your credibility.
You want customers to be able to WhatsApp or call you directly from the website — not fill lengthy enquiry forms.
You have a limited budget and want a high-quality professional result without paying for features you'll never use.
You operate primarily through walk-ins, word-of-mouth, or referrals — and want a website that supports that rather than replaces it.
You want to start simple and upgrade to the Growth plan later once your business scales.
