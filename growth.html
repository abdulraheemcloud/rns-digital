Home, About, Services (multiple pages), Gallery, Testimonials, FAQ, Blog (structure only), and Contact. More pages means more entry points from Google.

Conversion-Optimized Design

Strategic CTA placement on every page. Lead capture forms above the fold. Sticky contact buttons. Design decisions guided by what drives action, not just what looks good.

Multi-Point Lead Capture

Enquiry forms on landing pages, sidebar forms, WhatsApp floating button, click-to-call on mobile, and a dedicated "Get a Quote" or "Book a Consultation" flow.

05

Targeted keyword research for your business type and location. Optimized page titles, meta descriptions, heading hierarchy, structured data markup, and XML sitemap submission.

Google Analytics Integration

Track where your visitors come from, which pages they visit, and what they do. We'll set up Google Analytics and explain how to read the basic reports that matter.

07

Photo Gallery / Portfolio Section

A professional gallery to showcase your work, property listings, food photos, clinic infrastructure, or products — with a clean lightbox and mobile-optimised display.

08

Testimonials & Trust Section
