Domain & Hosting Clarity

What Happens After Year One?

Both our plans include assistance setting up your domain and hosting for the first year. From the second year onwards, you'll renew your domain directly with the registrar (like GoDaddy or BigRock) at approximately ₹800 – ₹1,200/year depending on the extension (.in, .com, etc.). Hosting renewals vary by provider — we'll guide you through the options.

We do not add any markup on renewals. You pay the registrar directly, and we have no involvement in that transaction.

No hidden ongoing fees from RNS Digital. After project delivery, the only recurring costs you'll have are your domain and hosting renewals — both paid directly to third-party providers at standard market rates.

FAQ

Common Pricing Questions

Is the pricing fixed or will there be extra charges?
The plan price is fixed. The only additional costs would be domain and hosting (which you pay to third-party providers) and any optional monthly support you choose to add. We'll always tell you upfront if your requirement falls outside the plan scope.
Can I upgrade from Basic to Growth later?
Yes. If you start with the Basic plan and want to upgrade to Growth, you can do so at any point. You'll pay the difference and we'll add the Growth features to your existing website.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept UPI, bank transfer (NEFT/RTGS/IMPS), and standard payment apps. Typically we take 50% upfront and 50% before the final handover.
Do you offer any payment in installments?
Our standard structure is 50% advance + 50% on delivery. For the Growth plan, we can discuss a milestone-based payment schedule during the consultation.
What if I need more pages than the plan includes?
Additional pages can be added at ₹1,500 per page for Basic or ₹2,000 per page for Growth. We'll quote this transparently before starting work.
