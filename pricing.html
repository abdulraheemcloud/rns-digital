What Happens After Year One?

Both plans include your domain and hosting setup for the 1st year at no extra cost. From the 2nd year, you'll renew your domain directly with the registrar (like GoDaddy or BigRock) at approximately ₹800 – ₹1,200/year. Hosting renewals depend on provider.

We do not mark up these costs. You pay the registrar directly. We are not involved.

No recurring charges from RNS Digital. After project delivery, the only ongoing cost is your domain renewal (~₹1,000/year) paid directly to the registrar. Zero hidden fees from us.

FAQ

Common Questions

Is the price fixed or will there be extra charges?
The plan price is fixed. No extra charges from us. Domain and hosting from 2nd year are paid by you directly to the provider — we don't touch that money.
Can I upgrade from Basic to Standard later?
Yes. You can upgrade any time by paying the difference (₹3,500). We'll add all Standard features to your existing website.
What payment methods do you accept?
UPI, bank transfer (NEFT/IMPS), and standard payment apps. We take 50% advance and 50% before final handover.
How long does delivery take?
Basic plan: 7 working days. Standard plan: 10–14 working days. Timeline starts once you share your content (logo, photos, text) and 50% advance is received.
Is the monthly support compulsory?
No. Monthly support is completely optional. Your website runs independently without it. Choose it only if you want regular updates or ongoing help.
